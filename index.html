About Me

I’m a final-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich, advised by Mrinmaya Sachan and Antoine Bosselut. Previously, I completed my MPhil at CUHK and my Bachelor’s at HUST.

I’m currently on the academic job market (2025–2026), seeking tenure-track positions starting Fall 2026. I welcome opportunities for research visits, talks, and collaborations—feel free to reach out!

Research Philosophy

I study how AI systems can perceive the world as humans do and reason in ways we understand. My work focuses on visual language, interpretability, and reasoning. I believe trustworthy AI must be grounded in real-world perception and have transparent internal mechanisms.
